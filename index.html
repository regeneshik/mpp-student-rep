My Platform

Direct Democracy

Every substantive decision, resolution, or public statement will go to the student body first. I will hold regular, structured open assemblies. No more decisions made behind closed doors by 11 people in the name of 855.

Real Constitution Reform

The rules failed us. I will push for mandatory student consultation periods and supermajority thresholds before any resolution touching political or reputational matters can pass.

Curriculum & Careers

AI in Academia
